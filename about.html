About Us

We are a group of JMU students from Richmond, Virginia Beach, and Maryland, who formed our band in the spring of 2020. We got together and began our journey shortly before the pandemic. The quarentine and time away from normal life gave us plenty of time to practice and become tight as a band and as friends. Since things have started to get back to normal, we have played countless cover shows to fund our equipment as well as studio time. Also giving us time to write our E.P. which is to be release in early August of 2022. We look foward for you all to hear it!
